Introduction

Welcome to IP TV ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe experience when using our application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our IP TV mobile application.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. By using the application, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Playlist URLs: M3U playlist URLs that you manually add to the application
  • Favorites: Channels you mark as favorites
  • Custom Playlists: Playlists you create within the app
  • App Settings: Your preferences such as theme, language, and quality settings

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App usage statistics, viewing history (stored locally)
  • Network Information: IP address and network type for streaming purposes

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the application's functionality
  • To load and stream IPTV channels
  • To save your preferences and favorites locally on your device
  • To display Electronic Program Guide (EPG) information
  • To improve the application's performance and user experience
  • To diagnose technical issues and bugs

Data Storage

All user data including favorites, playlists, and settings are stored locally on your device. We do not upload or store your personal viewing data on external servers.

  • Favorites and playlists are stored in a local SQLite database
  • Settings are stored using Android SharedPreferences
  • No personal data is transmitted to our servers

Third-Party Services

Our application may interact with the following third-party services:

  • IPTV Stream Sources: Publicly available M3U playlists from iptv-org.github.io
  • EPG Providers: Electronic Program Guide data from external XML sources
  • Channel Logos: Images hosted on third-party servers

We do not control and are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • The app uses HTTPS for all network communications
  • We do not collect or transmit sensitive personal information

Children's Privacy

Our application does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Some IPTV channels may contain content not suitable for children. Parental guidance is advised.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: View all your stored data within the app
  • Delete: Clear all app data through Android settings or within the app
  • Modify: Edit your favorites and playlists at any time
  • Export: Your playlists can be exported as M3U files

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app and updating the "Last updated" date.

Disclaimer

IP TV is a media player application that plays publicly available IPTV streams. We do not host, upload, or distribute any video content. All streams are sourced from publicly available M3U playlists. Users are responsible for ensuring they have the right to access the content they stream.
